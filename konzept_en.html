I. Framework Conditions

1. Presentation of the Institution

“SprachRaum Kaufbeuren” is operated by the municipal authority “City of Kaufbeuren” and is a stationary intercultural institution. It is located in the Neugablonz district, which is part of Kaufbeuren. The institution offers appropriate support for people of all ages. It places special emphasis on integration and social interaction. The location was deliberately chosen to ensure a wide range of leisure activities in the immediate surroundings. The institution also places great value on good access to municipal infrastructure.

2. Structure of the Institution

The language center has a well-thought-out spatial and organizational structure that creates optimal conditions for the care of children, adolescents, and young adults.

2.1 Facilities:

  • Total of 16 rooms:
  • Parents’ café: counter, seating, children’s play area, fresh meals
  • Homework support room with tables and chairs
  • Nursing room (for breastfeeding babies)
  • Prayer rooms: Muslim, Christian, and one flexible room
  • Language rooms: 2 rooms, also used by the social worker
  • Common room: seating, foosball, billiards, drinks counter, sound system
  • Team room for meetings
  • Speech therapy room: option for speech therapy courses
  • Sports hall: various sports equipment, goals, balls
  • Outdoor area: used instead of the sports hall in good weather, large meadow
  • Sports field and small pond
  • Restrooms: 4 gender-neutral restrooms, one women’s restroom

2.2 Location and Surroundings

  • The institution is located in an urban area bordering a forest
  • Leisure facilities such as ponds, sports areas, a youth center, and a playground are easily accessible on foot
  • Schools, medical practices, shopping facilities, and sports activities such as handball, swimming, dancing, or martial arts are well connected by public transportation

2.3 Operating and Opening Hours

Regular opening hours: 7:00 a.m. – 8:00 p.m.
Consultation hours: Tuesday – Thursday 8:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m., later by appointment
Care provided: 300 days per year

2.4 Staff Structure

  • 5 educators / social workers / special needs caregivers
  • 8 assistant / support staff
  • 3 director
  • 1 cook
  • 1 language specialist
  • 1 speech therapist
  • 3 internship positions
  • 1 social worker
  • 1 housekeeping staff member
  • 1 sign language specialist

2.5 Financing

The financing of our institution is based on a combination of private and public funds:

  • Public funding from the city
  • Donations from foundations
  • Parental contributions
  • Income from internal institutional activities
  • Voluntary helpers

II. Mission Statement

1. Visions and Values

1.1 Nutrition

Vision: “Our vision is a world in which conscious, sustainable, and enjoyable nutrition forms the foundation for health, quality of life, and responsibility toward people and nature.”

Mission statement: At our institution “SprachRaum Kaufbeuren,” nutrition represents more than just food intake – it is an expression of culture, community, and mindfulness. We believe that knowledge about healthy and sustainable nutrition empowers people to act independently and responsibly. We aim to create learning environments where enjoyment, education, and awareness of personal health and the environment grow together.

1.2 Freedom of Religion

Vision: “A world in which every person can freely choose, practice, or reject their religion – with mutual respect, peace, and without fear of discrimination.”

Mission statement: At our institution “SprachRaum Kaufbeuren,” freedom of religion is central to respectful coexistence. We believe that open dialogue about language, culture, and faith fosters understanding and tolerance. This enables individual and social development in which diversity is experienced and lived as a strength.

1.3 Participation

Vision: “All visitors to our institution are entitled to actively shape their language learning process and adapt it to their individual pace.”

Mission statement: At our institution “SprachRaum Kaufbeuren,” participation is a core principle. Everyone has the opportunity to help shape the institution and use it according to their individual needs. This forms the basis for positive development and enjoyment in learning languages.

III. Professional Concept

1. Target Groups

People of all ages and backgrounds are welcome to join us if they wish to learn a language or need support (German, English, sign language, French, Spanish). Tutoring, language courses, and activities with a focus on language are offered. During language courses, parents have the option to use free childcare services for their children.
