Programs

Language Courses and Tutoring:

  • German for all ages
  • English
  • French
  • Spanish
  • Sign Language
  • Individual support (tutoring)
  • Learning spaces promoting participation and self-directed learning

Care and Leisure Activities

  • Homework supervision for children
  • Parents’ café with games, exchange opportunities, and healthy meals
  • Common room: foosball, billiards, music system, drink bar
  • Gym for sports activities
  • Outdoor area: lawn, sports field, small pond

Religious and Cultural Offers

  • Prayer rooms for Muslims, Christians, and flexible space
  • Promotion of interculturality, integration, and tolerance
  • Offers strengthening religious freedom and respect

Additional Offers

  • Speech therapy: room for courses
  • Childcare during language courses
  • Participation: visitors can manage their own learning process
  • Internship opportunities for young adults
